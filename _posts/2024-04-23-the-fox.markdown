The fox etched red his fur <br>
among and tensed the mist, <br>
a wanderer astir <br>
the reeds which heaven kisst <br>
and thou amidst <br>
didst <br>
list <br>
thy curvature: <br>

impure but ever grey among the weeds <br>
a gap in stead the light which now recedes. <br>
